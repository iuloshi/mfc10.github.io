AmeriCorps Alums Chapter Network

A national network of engaged citizens and civic leaders.

AmeriCorps Alums supports 70 chapters across the country led by chapter leaders. Though this online network, chapters and their leaders can learn more about the national organization and find resources and a peer group to support their chapter.
